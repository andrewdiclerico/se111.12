Introduction

Saving Private Ryan Is a film produced in 1998 directed by Steven Spielberg and written bu Robert Rodat. Set during the Invasion of Normandy in World War Two.


The film follow United States Army Rangers Captain John H. Miller and his squad as they search for a paratrooper.


The paratrooper is Private First Class James Francis Ryan. James Ryan is the last surviving brother of four, the other three being killed in action.


The goal for Captain Miller and his squad is to bring Private Ryan home to his mother to ease the pain and suffering cause from the loss of her three other sons.
